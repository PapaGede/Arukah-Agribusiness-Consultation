EXPERIENCE IN THIS PASSION

Arukah Vittles Limited is an agribusiness food-processing firm that provides access to a nutritious range of branded tiger nuts products to the upscale market.

The business started in 2015, incorporated the same year under the Ghana Companies Code (1963) Act 179 as an Enterprise, and transitioned into a company in 2017 with pre-seed investment from Accelerator Program run by Innohub Limited in Accra.
